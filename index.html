About Us

A luminous corner of coastal Bulgaria

Stella was imagined as more than a menu — it is an atmosphere grounded in warmth: ember-toned accents, terraces that catch the salt air, and a kitchen that honours both flame and finesse.

Our guests wander in from the Sunny Beach corridor and quiet Sveti Vlas evenings alike. We greet them with craft cocktails, attentive service, and plates that spotlight Black Sea seafood alongside fire-kissed grills — the heart of our house.

Whether you are here for a long holiday table or an intimate two-top, we reserve the same care: unhurried pacing, generous hospitality, and a setting that feels quietly cinematic.
